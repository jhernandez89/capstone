Semi-structured Interview

Semi-structured interviews are a hybrid between unstructued and structured models

How does this work?


In a semi-structured interview, we are going to prepare interview questions in advance, but we will allow the participant to steer the majoirty of the interview. They will be able to explore topics indirectly related to the question

Semi-structured interviews are about the participant's experience

Ask yourself,

What information are you trying to find out?

What concepts or themes relate to your topic?

How could the topic have possibly affected the participant's life?

These kind of questions are good to ask yourself in order to get a better idea of the kind of questions you should ask your participants. Remember, the more meaningful and thoughtout your questions are, the more likely you'll get meaningful responses from your participants

Creating questions for your participants might seem easy, but in reality the way in which you ask the question is very important. Students in graduate school will spend weeks and sometimes months just creating their questions

We don't want you spending months on this, but we would like to help you think through your questions a little. After all, we don't want to spend hours interviewing people with a questionaire that isn't going to give us a lot of meaningful data
