Semi-structured Interview

Create Project

Semi-structured interviews are a hybrid between unstructured and structured interview models.

Typically, questions are prepared beforehand, but the participant is allowed to answer and explore the question freely even if this means visiting indirectly related topics to the given question

Pros

  • A good hybrid which adopts some structure, making it easier to compare data accross participants while allowing participants some freedom to personalize their response to their experiences

Cons

  • Traditionally not as easy to compare data accross participants as structured interviews
